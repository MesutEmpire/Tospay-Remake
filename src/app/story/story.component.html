The greater purpose

We develop tools to help unlock the growth
potential of African business

Globalization and rapid change in technology have redefined how
we do business. This could not be more true in Africa where radical
change due to mobile banking and mobile money have redefined
how we make and receive payments, forcing organizations to rethink
how they develop their solutions.

At TosPay we help you define your unique business problem and
create innovative custom solutions that seamlessly integrate into you
organization, letting you expose your products and services to
greater markets, powered by our secure, instant and unified
payments system.
